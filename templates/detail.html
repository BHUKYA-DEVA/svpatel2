At S.V. PATEL, we are committed to transforming ordinary spaces into extraordinary experiences. Whether designing an intimate interior or crafting a grand exterior, our focus is on blending aesthetics with functionality. We believe that every space should not only look stunning but also serve the practical needs of its occupants
What Sets Us Apart With our Exclusive Design service, we move beyond traditional design approaches, offering you custom-made interiors and exteriors that reflect luxury, elegance, and innovation. Every design is a signature piece, designed to not only meet but exceed your expectations.
Tailored to You: We begin by understanding your vision, preferences, and lifestyle. From here, our team of world-class designers curates a design that is uniquely yours.
Luxurious Materials: We source the finest materials from around the world, ensuring that each element of your design is of the highest quality. Whether it’s custom furniture, rare stones, or designer textiles, your space will exude luxury.
Attention to Detail: From architectural features to the smallest design accents, no detail is too small. Our meticulous approach guarantees perfection in every aspect of your design.

Expertise in Both Worlds
Interior Design: Our interior design services focus on creating harmonious, stylish, and functional environments. From selecting the perfect color schemes to choosing unique furniture pieces and decor, we aim to make every room reflect your personal taste and lifestyle.
Key aspects of our interior design include:
Personalized space planning to optimize functionality and flow. Custom furniture and décor selection to fit your style. Lighting designs that enhance mood and ambience. Use of sustainable materials and innovative technologies.
Exterior Design: First impressions matter, and our exterior design services are all about enhancing curb appeal while ensuring durability and elegance. Whether it’s a residential home or a commercial property, our exterior designs seamlessly merge architectural integrity with visual impact.
