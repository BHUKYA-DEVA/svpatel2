OWNER

S.V. Patel and Co. was founded by Shri S.V. , a visionary entrepreneur with a passion for transforming spaces. With decades of experience in the interior and exterior design industry, Shri Patel has always prioritized quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. His keen eye for detail and dedication to sourcing premium materials has made S.V. Patel and Co. a trusted name in the industry.
